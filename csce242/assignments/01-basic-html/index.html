How Music Unites Us

Afro Beat is a West African music genre that is a child to funk, jazz, and other soul influences.
Dreamville is a concert that was created by an artist named J. Cole. For my seventeenth birthday
I went to the concert for the first time and thats when I first heard Afro Beat live. That was the
first time where I saw people of all different type of ethnicities and backgrounds dancing together.

Coming from a culture where it is prodominatly African American I did not see much of
this in my childhood. It was very exciting to see people step out of the box musically which
I felt, lead to unification. While I was at the concert, people with different backgrounds would spark
up a conversation with me and would share stories about how they were introduced to Afro Beat or
how they felt when they first heard it. In conclusion, Afro Beat is a genre of music that can bring
people together and connect us all in a unique way.
